DC Motor Assembly Reverse Engineering

Reverse Engineering & Drawing Approval · Oct 2025 – Nov 2025

Context: Performed a comprehensive teardown and analysis in MUN's Production Technology lab to translate a physical, legacy DC motor assembly into high-fidelity, manufacturing-ready documentation without existing reference blueprints.

Execution: Disassembled and measured physical components using precision instruments.
